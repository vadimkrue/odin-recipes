Oatmeal porridge

Description

Oatmeal is a warm, comforting dish made from rolled oats that are simmered in water or milk until soft and creamy. It's a versatile and nutritious breakfast option, rich in fiber and known for its ability to keep you full and energized throughout the morning. The base is simple, but it can be customized with a variety of toppings like fresh fruit, nuts, or sweeteners such as honey or maple syrup, making it a perfect canvas for both sweet and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats;
  • 2 cups water or milk;
  • Pinch of salt;
  • Honey fruits, nuts, cinnamon if you want.

Steps

  1. Pour 2 cups of water or milk into a medium saucepan and bring to a boil,
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling liquid,
  3. Stir in 1 cup of rolled oats,
  4. Reduce heat to low and let the mixture simmer,
  5. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking,
  6. Once the desired consistency is reached, remove the oatmeal from heat,
  7. Let the oatmeal rest for 1 minute before serving,
  8. Add your favorite toppings such as honey, fruits, nuts, or cinnamon.
