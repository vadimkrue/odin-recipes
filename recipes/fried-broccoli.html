Fried broccoli

Description

Fried broccoli is a quick and flavorful dish where broccoli florets are sautéed until tender and lightly crisp. This method of cooking enhances the natural flavors of the broccoli while keeping it vibrant and nutritious. Perfect as a side dish or a light meal, fried broccoli can be enjoyed on its own or seasoned with garlic, lemon, or herbs for added zest. You can also toss in some grated cheese or nuts for extra texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli (cut into florets);
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or butter);
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced, optional);
  • Salt and pepper to taste;
  • Optional: lemon juice, grated cheese, or chili flakes for extra flavor.

Steps

  1. Cut the broccoli into small florets and rinse them under cold water,
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat,
  3. Add the broccoli florets to the pan, stirring occasionally,
  4. After 2-3 minutes, add minced garlic (if using) and continue to sauté,
  5. Cook the broccoli for about 5-7 minutes until it turns tender and slightly browned,
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste,
  7. Remove from heat and, if desired, drizzle with lemon juice or sprinkle with cheese or chili flakes before serving.
