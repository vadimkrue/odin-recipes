Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a simple yet satisfying dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them gently in a pan until soft and fluffy. Theyâ€™re a versatile option for breakfast or any meal of the day. Easy to prepare, scrambled eggs can be enhanced with various add-ins like cheese, herbs, vegetables, or meats. Their light and creamy texture makes them a comforting, protein-packed option to start your day with energy.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs;
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil;
  • Salt and pepper to taste;
  • Optional: cheese, herbs, vegetables, or meats for extra flavor.

Steps

  1. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well blended,
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat,
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan, allowing them to spread evenly,
  4. Let the eggs cook for a few seconds without stirring,
  5. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, pushing them from the edges toward the center,
  6. Continue cooking and stirring until the eggs are soft and slightly runny (or firmer if preferred),
  7. Remove the eggs from the heat just before they are fully set,
  8. Season with salt and pepper, and add any desired toppings like cheese, herbs, or vegetables.
